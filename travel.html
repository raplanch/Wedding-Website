getting to knightdale
Raleigh-Durham International Airport The Raleigh-Durham Airport serves the Triangle area of North Carolina. The airport is approximately 22 minutes from downtown Raleigh and 26 minutes from our venue. Both Uber and Lyft serve this airport. Amtrak The train service Amtrak has a station in the heart of downtown Raleigh. The Raleigh Union Station is approximately 30 minutes from our venue.
We can’t wait
to see you! Please keep in mind that October is a busy time of year in the Triangle. We would recommend that you make travel and accommodation plans accordingly.

good to know
Day-of Transportation We’d like to remind everyone to please travel responsibly. There will be no shuttle transportation between the venue and the hotels. We recommend carpooling or ride-sharing. Uber and Lyft are both within range of our venue.
