What should I wear? We ask guests to please join us in cocktail attire. Cocktail attire is semi-formal attire that is dressy but not overly formal. For women, this could include a dress or a skirt or pants paired with a nice top. Men can opt for a suit or dress pants with a dress shirt and jacket. The goal is to look polished and stylish without being overly formal.
May I bring a plus one? We’re excited to celebrate with everyone who can make it, but please bring a plus one only if their name is listed on your invitation.
Are children allowed at the wedding? If your children are invited, their names will be included in your invitation. We kindly request that only those indicated on the invitation attend. 
How do I RSVP, and by what date? We kindly ask all guests to RSVP online by September 9th. The invitation will include a link for your convenience.
Is transportation provided to and from the venue? We will not be providing a shuttle service. Please plan your transportation in advance and drive responsibly. Uber and Lyft are both within range of our venue.
What is the parking situation at the venue? There is a large parking lot at the entrance to the venue. Guests will be instructed to park there and will be shuttled to the venue at the back of the property by a bus.
Will the ceremony, cocktail hour,
or reception be held outside? We plan to hold the ceremony and cocktail hour outside, weather permitting. There is also a lawn area that will be staged with casual seating and yard games, as well as a fire pit for guests to enjoy during the reception.
Is there anything I need to know
about the food or beverage service? There will be catered appetizers at cocktail hour, but we'll be offering two food truck options instead of traditional catering for dinner. Guests will be asked to indicate their food truck preference when they RSVP.
